REGULATORY AND NON-CUSTODY NOTICE: A2A Global Inc ("A2A Global") is a marketplace and software platform operator. A2A Global does not resell financial services, does not engage in money transmission as defined under the Bank Secrecy Act (31 U.S.C. §§ 5311–5332), FinCEN regulations (31 CFR § 1010.100(ff)(5)), or any applicable state money transmission law. 1. Scope, Role, and Regulatory Status

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how A2A Global Inc ("A2A Global," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use the A2A Global marketplace and software Platform as a Payer (Client/Buyer), Receiver (Freelancer/Seller), or visitor.

A2A Global is a marketplace and software platform that develops, maintains, and operates proprietary technology to connect Payers (US corporate clients) with Receivers (freelancers and independent contractors) of professional services. The Platform facilitates access to financial services provided by independently licensed third parties ("Licensed Service Providers") but does not itself resell, provide, or engage in any regulated financial service, including money transmission as defined under 31 CFR § 1010.100(ff)(5).

A2A Global's data collection and processing practices described in this Policy are those of a technology and marketplace operator. A2A Global does not collect or process data in the capacity of a money transmitter, money services business, bank, or financial institution. To the extent Licensed Service Providers collect or process personal data in connection with regulated financial services, such collection and processing is governed by the Licensed Service Provider's own privacy policy and regulatory obligations.

2. Definitions

Capitalized terms used in this Policy have the same meaning as defined in the Terms of Use. Key definitions relevant to this Policy include:

"Platform Account" — An account held by A2A Global Inc with a duly Licensed Money Service Business or bank. When a Payer pays for Receiver services, funds are credited to the Platform Account. The Platform Account is held with and operated by the Licensed Service Provider under its own license and regulatory obligations. A2A Global does not have custody, control, or beneficial ownership over funds in the Platform Account. The Licensed Service Provider is the sole custodian and controller of funds in the Platform Account at all times.

"Payer Account" — A virtual account within the A2A Global Platform assigned to a registered Payer. The Payer Account is an informational and record-keeping feature of the Platform software that displays transaction details, project information, invoices, milestone statuses, and payment histories. It does NOT constitute a bank account, deposit account, e-money account, stored-value account, or any type of financial account. No funds are held in the Payer Account.

"Receiver Account" — A virtual account within the A2A Global Platform assigned to a registered Receiver. The Receiver Account is an informational and record-keeping feature of the Platform software that displays earnings information, milestone statuses, payout histories, and transactional data. It does NOT constitute a bank account, deposit account, e-money account, stored-value account, or any type of financial account. No funds are held in the Receiver Account.

"Provider Account" — An account held by a Payer or Receiver directly with a Licensed Service Provider, governed entirely by the Licensed Service Provider's own terms, conditions, privacy policies, and regulatory obligations.

"Platform Fee" — The fee charged by A2A Global as remuneration for the use of its proprietary marketplace and software Platform, including matching, project management, milestone tracking, collaboration tools, and the facilitation of connections between Payers and Receivers. The Platform Fee is consideration for technology and marketplace services, not for money transmission, fund custody, or financial services.

3. What A2A Global Does NOT Do — Data and Regulatory Exclusions

Consistent with A2A Global's status as a marketplace and software platform operator (not a money transmitter, money services business, bank, or financial institution), A2A Global expressly does NOT:

1. Process, hold, custody, control, or settle financial transactions or funds of any kind. All fund flows are managed exclusively by Licensed Service Providers through the Platform Account and Provider Accounts.

2. Collect or process personal data in the capacity of a money transmitter, money services business, or financial institution as defined under 31 CFR § 1010.100(ff) or any applicable state law.

3. Resell, re-offer, or intermediate any financial or banking service. The Platform facilitates access to such services as a technology intermediary only.

4. Store payment card numbers, bank account details, routing numbers, or settlement data on its own systems. Such data is collected and stored exclusively by Licensed Service Providers and their sub-processors.

5. Act as a controller of financial transaction data for purposes of BSA/AML regulatory compliance. Licensed Service Providers are independently responsible for their own data protection, BSA/AML compliance, and regulatory reporting related to fund flows.

6. Sell, rent, license, or trade personal data to third parties for their own marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

7. Use personal data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects concerning you, unless explicitly disclosed and subject to your consent.

8. Act as an employer or staffing agency. A2A Global does not process payroll, employment taxes, benefits, or employment-related data on behalf of Receivers. Receivers are independent contractors.

9. Retain personal data longer than necessary for the purposes described in this Policy and applicable legal obligations.

4. Regulatory Defense Provisions — Data Processing Context

A2A Global's personal data collection and processing activities described in this Policy occur exclusively in the context of marketplace and software platform operations. These activities are further contextualized by the regulatory defense provisions set forth in Section 5 of the Terms of Use, which are incorporated by reference. Key data-related implications of those provisions include:

Integral-to-Sale Exemption (31 CFR § 1010.100(ff)(5)(ii)(F)): Any transactional data that A2A Global processes (project postings, milestone statuses, amounts, timestamps, references) is processed as part of marketplace and project management operations integral to the sale of professional services — not as part of a separate money transmission service.

Technology Provider Exemption (31 CFR § 1010.100(ff)(5)(ii)(A)): A2A Global processes data in its capacity as a technology and network access provider to Licensed Service Providers. Data exchanged between A2A Global and Licensed Service Providers (e.g., transaction instructions, status updates, fee calculations) is transmitted in A2A Global's capacity as a software platform, not as a money transmitter.

No Custody / No Control: A2A Global does not process or store actual fund custody data. The Platform Account is held by and under the control of the Licensed Service Provider. A2A Global receives only transactional metadata (amounts, timestamps, statuses) for display in Payer Accounts and Receiver Accounts — not fund custody or settlement data.

Licensed Service Provider Independence: Licensed Service Providers collect and process their own data (including sensitive financial data such as bank account numbers, card numbers, and settlement details) under their own privacy policies and regulatory obligations. A2A Global is not a joint controller or processor of such data with the Licensed Service Provider for BSA/AML or financial regulatory purposes.

Platform Fee Data: Records of Platform Fees charged, deducted, and paid to A2A Global are processed as revenue records for marketplace and technology services — not as money transmission records.

Communications and Content: Messages sent via Platform messaging tools, support requests, feedback, notices, and files or documents uploaded in connection with projects or support interactions.
